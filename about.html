About The Weeknd

The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and producer known for his dark, atmospheric sound and cinematic storytelling.

From underground mixtapes to global superstardom, his music captures heartbreak, fame, desire, and reinvention.
